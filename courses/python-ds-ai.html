Python for Data Science & AI

Course Overview: This course is tailored for aspiring data scientists, analysts, and AI developers who want to use Python as the foundation for solving real-world business and research problems through data analysis, visualization, and machine learning.

Who Should Take This Course?

  • Beginners or intermediate Python users looking to specialize in Data Science
  • Engineering & IT students who want to pursue a career in AI & ML
  • Working professionals looking to transition into data-focused roles
  • Business analysts or enthusiasts aiming to gain predictive analytics skills

Course Benefits

  • Hands-on projects involving real-world datasets and business use-cases
  • Model building using Scikit-learn, Pandas, NumPy, and Matplotlib
  • Mini Capstone Projects & Resume-oriented ML assignments
  • Career grooming, mock interviews, and job referral assistance

Topics Covered

  • Python Refresher for Data Science
  • Data Wrangling with Pandas & NumPy
  • Data Visualization using Matplotlib & Seaborn
  • Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
  • Machine Learning Basics with Scikit-learn
  • Supervised & Unsupervised Learning Models
  • Model Evaluation & Optimization
  • Introduction to Deep Learning & Neural Networks

Tools & Platforms

  • Jupyter Notebook
  • Google Colab
  • Python Libraries: Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, Matplotlib, Seaborn

Real-World Applications

This course opens doors to jobs in data analysis, machine learning engineering, business intelligence, and research roles across industries like healthcare, finance, and e-commerce.
