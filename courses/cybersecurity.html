Cybersecurity Essentials for All

Course Overview: This beginner-friendly course is designed to help anyone understand cybersecurity basics and how to stay safe online. Whether you're an employee, student, or digital user, this course will empower you with critical cyber hygiene knowledge.

Who Should Take This Course?

  • General public looking to protect their digital identity
  • Students entering into tech or security fields
  • Professionals using the internet for work or finance
  • Parents concerned about kids' digital safety

Course Benefits

  • Understand common cyber threats and attacks
  • Learn how to secure personal and professional data
  • Recognize phishing, scams, and social engineering techniques
  • Develop good password, email, and browsing hygiene

Topics Covered

  • What is Cybersecurity?
  • Types of Cyber Threats (Phishing, Malware, Ransomware)
  • Password Management & Two-Factor Authentication
  • Safe Browsing & Wi-Fi Security
  • Email Safety and Recognizing Scams
  • Social Media and Data Privacy
  • Cybersecurity for Kids & Families
  • Cyber Laws and Ethics

Tools and Techniques You'll Learn

  • Password managers (e.g., Bitwarden, LastPass)
  • Safe browsing tools (VPNs, HTTPS)
  • Antivirus software and firewalls
  • Security settings for phones and laptops

Real-World Applications

Cybersecurity is no longer just an IT concern — it's essential for everyone. Whether you’re protecting personal accounts, business data, or educating children, this course empowers you with skills for safer digital living.
